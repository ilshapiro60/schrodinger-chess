Features

Chess, but deeper

Everything you love about chess, plus a whole new dimension — literally.

♟
Multi-board gameplay
Play on 2 or 3 stacked 8×8 boards simultaneously. Each board starts with a full chess setup for both sides.
⬆
Piece shifting
After each move, teleport one of your pieces to an adjacent board. Shifting is circular — pieces wrap from the top board back to the bottom.
👑
Multi-king battle
Each player has one king per board. Capture all your opponent's kings to win. Losing one king doesn't end the game.
🤖
AI opponent
Play against a built-in AI using minimax with alpha-beta pruning. Great for practice or solo play.
🌐
Online multiplayer
Challenge a friend anywhere in the world using a 6-letter room code. No account required.
🎨
6 beautiful themes
Midnight Ocean, Gothic Crimson, Emerald Forest, Classic Wood, Purple Night, and Arctic Ice.

How to Play

Simple to learn,
deep to master

Every turn has two phases.

1

Choose a board and move

Pick any of the 2 or 3 boards and make any legal chess move on it. Pieces move normally within their board — no vertical movement on your own.

2

Shift a piece

After moving, you may teleport one of your non-king pieces from that same board up or down to an adjacent board. The destination square must be empty. You can skip the shift.

3

Capture all kings to win

A king is captured when an opponent's piece moves onto its square. The first player to lose all their kings loses the game.

★

Strategy tip

Shift pieces to boards where your opponent's king is vulnerable. A well-timed shift can open a deadly attack on a board your opponent thought was safe.
